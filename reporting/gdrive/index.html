Google Drive Privacy Risk

This security / privacy risk is more user error then anything. it relies on google drive allowing you to search for any document shared to you either by accident or on purpose by the whole district. You can easily see this by going google drive and searching source:domain

This exposes alot of documents, some which were clearly not meant to be shared to the whole district from students and teachers.

The only real way to mitigate would just to inform staff/students about the implications of sharing and to ensure they arent selecting the option of sharing to everyone in the district.
